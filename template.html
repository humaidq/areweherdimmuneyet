Explanation

The goal of @goal% of the people fully vaccinated is based on the estimated R0 rate of the delta variant. Estimates show the R0 of the delta variant is between 4-9, we assume an R0 of 6.5.

This estimate is predicted using polynomial regression, expect it to change frequently. Remember, this is just an estimation on an arbitrary goal.
